Locate the Deltarune files

Deltamod will need to modify the various data.win files in order to add mods to your game. You will now setup Deltamod to point to your Deltarune installation.

Deltarune folder:




Deltamod will copy your installation into its app data folder in order to be able to apply mods to it. You may add more installs later.

...or, alternatively, you can download the latest Deltarune demo directly from Deltamod.


Deltarune will be downloaded from the itch.io page. To import Chapters 3&4, you must use your own installation. Deltamod doesn't condone piracy.
